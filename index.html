Welcome to Dice Game

Roll the dice as many times as you want, when you are satisfied hold the score to add it to your total score and pass the turn to the other player. Seems easy, right?

Here is the catch! If you get 1, your current score becomes 0 thus total score remains unchanged. Also the turn passes to the other player.

Don't get too greedy...ok?
